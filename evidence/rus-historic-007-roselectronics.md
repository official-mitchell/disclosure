Western aerospace engineers were skeptical — plasma stealth had been theorized for decades but never successfully implemented.

1. Russia's documented aerospace research doesn't show the theoretical foundation for this
2. The power requirements for sustained plasma generation are **enormous**
3. Russian aircraft haven't historically demonstrated the materials science needed
4. Yet... Russian systems keep showing up that shouldn't exist
